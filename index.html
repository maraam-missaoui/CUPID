✦ Who We Are

Cupid & Co. was built for dreamers

We started as a small passion project — two girls who were tired of settling for boring accessories. We wanted cute, affordable, aesthetic pieces that actually felt special.

Now we curate limited-edition jewelry, gift sets, and accessories for girls who want their style to tell a story. Every piece we pick is something we'd personally wear or gift to our best friend.

Based in Tunisia 🇹🇳 — shipping all over with love, and always with payment on delivery because trust matters.
